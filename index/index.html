ONE HEART

Our organization arose following the "Iron Swords" war. We saw the fundamental need for help for the families of the abducted, bereaved families and combatants with post-trauma.

We in the organization believe in mutual guarantee, therefore if someone needs help we are here for them, and therefore there is also the ability to visit us for people who feel the need. financially and personally

ptsd

Many of our dear soldiers who fought in the "Iron Swords" war have returned to us after a long battle with Ptsd and we are here to recognize and help them with what they need dear soldier you are not alone we are with you.

The families of the abductees

The families of the abductees who are currently going through a very, very difficult war to bring their children home face a lot of stress and anxiety throughout the war. We in the organization want to do our best to get through this period as easily as possible. We are with you.

Bereaved families

Bereaved families who sacrificed so much during the fighting, their children or their parents who went to fight for our country face a lot of sadness and loneliness and we are here to help them with what we can together we will win!
